Let’s walk through the most common and effective tournament layout scenarios for a 9-team age group. Each layout balances fairness, game count, and scheduling efficiency. I’ll break them down by pool structure, game count, and playoff format.
⚙️ Scenario 1: 3 Pools of 3 Teams
➤ Pool Play:
3 pools (A, B, C), 3 teams each
Each team plays 2 games (vs others in their pool)
Total Pool Games: 3 per pool × 3 = 9 games
➤ Playoffs:
Option A: Top 6 Advance
Quarterfinals: 3v6, 4v5 → 2 games
Semifinals: 1 vs winner 4/5, 2 vs winner 3/6 → 2 games
Final: 1 game
Total Playoff Games: 5
Option B: Top 4 Advance
Semifinals: 1v4, 2v3 → 2 games
Final: 1 game
Total Playoff Games: 3
Consolation: 3 teams not advancing → 1–2 games
⚙️ Scenario 2: 1 Pool of 9 Teams (Round Robin)
➤ Pool Play:
Each team plays 8 games
Total Games: (9 × 8) ÷ 2 = 36 games
➤ Playoffs:
Top 2 play Final → 1 game
Or Top 4: Semis + Final → 3 games
✅ Pros: Very fair, everyone plays everyone
❌ Cons: Too many games for a short weekend
⚙️ Scenario 3: 2 Pools (5 + 4 Teams)
➤ Pool Play:
Pool A (5 teams): 10 games (each team plays 4)
Pool B (4 teams): 6 games (each team plays 3)
Total Pool Games: 16
➤ Playoffs:
Option A: Top 2 from each pool
Semifinals: A1 vs B2, B1 vs A2 → 2 games
Final: 1 game
Total Playoff Games: 3
Option B: Top 3 from each pool
Quarterfinals: A2 vs B3, B2 vs A3 → 2 games
Semifinals: A1 vs QF winner, B1 vs QF winner → 2 games
Final: 1 game
Total Playoff Games: 5
⚙️ Scenario 4: 3 Pools of 3 Teams + Crossover
➤ Pool Play:
Same as Scenario 1: 9 games
Add 1 crossover game per team (vs team from another pool)
Each team plays 3 games
Total Games: 9 (pool) + 9 (crossover) = 18 games
➤ Playoffs:
Top 4 or 6 advance (same as above)
Total Playoff Games: 3–5
✅ Pros: Everyone gets 3 games before playoffs
✅ Cons: Slightly more complex to schedule
⚙️ Scenario 5: Flights Instead of Pools
Flight A: Top 5 teams (based on rankings)
Flight B: Remaining 4 teams
Each flight plays round-robin:
Flight A: 10 games
Flight B: 6 games
Total Games: 16
Then:
Flight A Final: Top 2 → 1 game
Flight B Final: Top 2 → 1 game
Total Playoff Games: 2
✅ Pros: Good for tiered competition
✅ Cons: No crossover between flights
